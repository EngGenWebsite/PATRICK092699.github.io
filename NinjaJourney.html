About

Ninaja Journey

The Ninja journey is a 2D platform android game which has a one-sided camera angle or side-view camera angle, it is also a side scrolling platform game which the player is from the left going to the right. It has the story that the ancient power in the kingdom was stolen by the Samurai and the king was decided to hire a ninja and get the ancient power back to the kingdom, this is the beginning of the ninja journey.

  • Development Platform: Unity Game Engine
  • Supported Platform: Android
  • Language: C#
  • Development period: 4 Months
  • Dimension: 2D Game

Key Features

The main player ninja is enabled to jump to avoid the harmful obstacles and shooting shuriken to kill the enemy heading our way, the ninja also sliding if our way is too small. During the game there is a timer where this represents the score of what time you finish the level, you can collect the coin and this will add to your final score. There is also an object that the player enables to recover the health bar stat of ninja. The objective or main goal of the Ninja is to overcome the obstacle, kill enemy and reach the end of each level.
